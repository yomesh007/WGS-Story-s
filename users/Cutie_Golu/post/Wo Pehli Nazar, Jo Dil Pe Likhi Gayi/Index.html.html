"Wo Pehli Nazar, Jo Dil Pe Likhi Gayi"

"2020 ka ek din, 5th February tha. 18 saal ki Anaya apne college ke pehle saal ka maza le rahi thi — hostel life, new friends, aur wo excitement jo sirf nayi shuruat ke saath aati hai. Us din dupahar me uski roommate Mehak hostel ke kamre me aayi aur bola, “Chal, library chalte hain… notes print karwane hain.” Anaya ka mann nahi tha — light sa headache tha aur thoda mood off. Par Mehak ke “chal na yaar” wale tone me ek alag hi force hota tha. Toh Anaya maan gayi. Dono library gaye, kaam khatam hua, thoda canteen me maggi kha li… aur shaam dhalne lagi. Tab Mehak ne casually bola, “Arre, chal tujhe mere ek friend se milati hoon. Photography club ka hai… bohot mast banda hai.” Aur bas… wahi se wo moment aaya. Gate ke paas khada tha — black hoodie, camera neck pe, aur smile… jaise directly aankhon me lag rahi ho. Uska naam tha Aarav. Haath milaya toh jaane kyun ek second ke liye sab awaaz fade ho gayi. Mehak ne mischievous hansi ke saath kaha, “Tum log baat karo, main ek call karke aati hoon.” 5 minute ki wo random si baatein bhi alag feel de rahi thi. Aarav ki baaton me ek calmness thi… aur aankhon me wo “I’m listening” wala look. Jaise har word absorb kar raha ho. Chalte chalte hostel ke gate aaya. Uska hostel alag tha, toh pehle woh turn le gaya. Bas ek halka sa “Bye” bola, lekin uske tone me wo warmth thi jo seedha dil me utar jati hai. Raat ko Anaya ne phone uthaya… insta open kiya… aur socha “Search karu kya?” Par phir drop kar diya. “Kya sochega?” Dusri taraf Aarav bhi search me laga tha, par Anaya ka account private nahi… bilkul hi nahi tha — kyunki uska insta hi nahi tha. Do din baad Anaya ne first time insta banaya. Pehle follow kiya, phir ek chhota sa “Hi” bhej diya. Reply turant aaya: “Tum wahi Anaya ho na, library wale din wali?” “Yep,” Anaya ne reply kiya. Baat chalti gayi… ek din Aarav ne casually pooch liya, “Tum single ho?” “Yeah. Tum?” “Same.” Phir usne bola, “Agar main tumhe coffee date pe poochu toh tum aaogi?” Anaya ka dil ek second ke liye skip hua… usne bola, “Pata nahi.” Next message: “Toh consider this a date invite.” Anaya ne us din koi jawab nahi diya. Raat me Mehak ko bataya, toh Mehak ne bas muskura ke kaha, “Tera call hai… bas apna heart mat todna.” Next day, Anaya ne coffee pe haan keh diya. Aur bas, yahi se wo slow, soft aur thoda filmy si love story shuru hui… jo Anaya ke life ka sabse pyara chapter ban gayi. 💖
